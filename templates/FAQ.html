What is stop loss?

An order with a stop loss is one placed with a 'trigger price'. It is placed to minimize the losses and the order can be either for a purchase or a sale.

My buying/selling order is not getting placed.

Please check for the following, if:
- The volume you want to Buy is NOT more than 1/3rd of the total volume in the market.
- The volume you want to Sell is NOT more than 1/6th of the total volume in the market.
- You have sufficient balance to place the buy order.
- You have any pending orders that has reserved your cash balance.
- You are trying to trade penny stocks. According to the new rules, stocks priced below a certain amount, say for e.g. Rs. 10, cannot be traded at all.

When is my cash book entry updated? I am having problems with my cash book updation.

Your Cash book gets updated immediately after the execution of a trade. In case it doesn't, kindly recheck if your trade has been executed successfully. If problem persists, please notify us.
